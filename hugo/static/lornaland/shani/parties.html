Parties

Got something to celebrate? Looking for something different? Why not book a Pamper Party?

Pamper Parties are great for
Birthdays, Hen Nights, Girlie Nights, Work Parties... whatever the celebrations!

Are you looking for a different type of kids party? Make your child the envy of all her friends and book our
Little Princess Party.

How to Book

  • Pamper Parties are available for 5 to 10 guests.
  • Little Princess Parties are available from 6 to 16 years.
  • A 20% non-returnable deposit is required to confirm your booking.
  • All guests must choose their treatments at least 1 week prior to the party.
  • The balance must be paid on the night.

Hostess Tips

  • Asks guests to wear loose fitting comfortable clothing.
  • Provide non-alcoholic drinks alongside alcoholic drinks.
  • If you are providing food, keep it simple and prepare it in advance to allow you time to relax and enjoy the night.
  • Ensure rooms are warm, your body temperature drops as you relax.
