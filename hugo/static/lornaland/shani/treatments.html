Tanning

Everyone loves a tan and the safest way to achieve a healthy golden tan is to fake it!

We offer a range of tanning products in spray or cream to help you look and feel good.
Sol Glo, Fake It, Rio and St Tropez.

To help you get the best from your tan, which should last 4-7 days, we recommend the following;

Before Your Appointment

  • Exfoliate the skin the day before, pay particular attention to elbows, knees, ankles and feet.
  • Shave unwanted hair the day before.
  • Shower the morning of your tan.
  • DO NOT use any products; talc, deodorant, lotions, creams, etc.
  • Wear loose fitting clothing.

Aftercare: Getting the Best from your Tan

  • Moisturise daily.
  • Avoid long hot baths, over frequent showering and swimming.
  • Pat yourself dry.
  • DO NOT exfoliate after tanning.

Waxing

Waxing is a great way to get rid of unwanted hair.

We use NADS wax which unlike other waxes, is cold so there's no heating or burning your skin. The wax is a thick gel which melts with your body heat so it rinses off completely with water. NADS wax is also 100% natural. In fact it's so safe, you could eat it!

When you shave or use creams that dissolve the hair on the surface your hair grows back fast and coarse. NADS removes even the hair's root so re growth is softer, finer and slower.

Waxing works best when the hair is at least 3-5mm long.

We ask that you advise us is you are taking any Glycolic Acid, Dermatological treatments or are Diabetic.

Before Your Appointment

  • DO NOT shower or bath the morning of your appointment.
  • DO NOT exfoliate before waxing.

Aftercare

  • DO NOT shower or bath for 24 hours after treatment.
  • Moisturise Daily.
