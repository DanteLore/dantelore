Welcome to North East Nails.

Welcome

Busy life? Hectic work schedule? Family to look after?
Want to indulge yourself and have some beauty treatments but can't find the time to visit the salon?

North East Nails is a complete mobile nail and beauty service bringing a range of beauty treatments to the comfort of your own home.

Our beauty therapist is well equipped to call at your home and offer all the services of a salon without you having to leave the house.

North East Nails are based in Hebburn and serve the South Tyneside area: Hebburn, Jarrow, South Shields as well as Pelaw. We are also happy to travel further afield if required, please enquire for further details.
