Dan and Lorna's American Adventure 2005

Monday, November 01, 2004

The Route

Due to the cost of the flights, we've had a change of itinerary.

We are no longer visiting Washington DC. It's a shame, though all we were really interested in seeing was The White House.

Our new itinerary looks like this:
  • New York - 4 nights at The Carter Hotel
  • Boston - 3 nights at The Copley Square Hotel
  • Cooperstown -1 night at The Lyoncher B&B
  • Buffalo - 2 nights at The Holiday Inn Downtown
  • Las Vegas - 4 nights at The Bellagio Hotel
  • Death Valley - 1 night at Furnace Creek Ranch
  • Yosemite National Park - 4 nights at Curry Village
  • San Francisco - 4 nights at The Taylor Hotel
  • San Simeon - 1 night at The Silver Surf Motel
  • Los Angeles - 1 night at Travelodge Sunset/La Brea
  • San Diego - 3 nights at Best Western Bayside Inn
We've got some prices of hotels in other towns and cities but we're still searching the good cheap ones!
