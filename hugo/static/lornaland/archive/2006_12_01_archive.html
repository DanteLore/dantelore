Saturday, December 23, 2006

chips away...

We got Chips Away to come and fix Marvin's vandal scratch and my little bumper chip yesterday and what a mighty fine job they did!

Originally, I'd had a quote from Reading for the front scratch at around £170 + VAT but once i'd had my little accident we decided to try the North East branch to see if they were cheaper - and they were! £100 + VAT for both front and back.

So, they came to collect the car at 9.30am and it was returned back to the house by 12.30pm looking as good as new.

We'd defintely use them again!
