i've won a haircut...

Listening to Reading 107fm a couple of days ago, I suddenly had an impulse and decided to enter the competition to win a haircut at Saks in Reading. Well, i'm desperate for a trim before my hoildays and am too skint to pay for it!

Managed to get through and kept on hold but I didn't manage to be caller 2 or caller 6.

So yesterday, I thought i'd give it another try and I was caller 2! And on topof that, I got the question right, I won a haircut! Fantastic.

So today, I made Debbie ring in and guess what, she was lucky caller 8 and she won one too!

Yeah! Nice hair all round! ;o)

Sunday, July 17, 2005

trip to the races...

Dan came home from work on Friday with a dozen red roses, a bottle of champagne and a birthday cake for me :o) After enjoying the champagne in the garden, we headed off for a chinese meal at the Shanpan Chinese. Very yummy.

Yesterday was the trip to the races. After fretting all week about the weather, it turned out to be a lovely sunny day.

Me, Dan, Debbie and Jason arrived at the racecourse at 1.30pm to find Gareth, Minnie, Simon, Eloise and Johnson had already arrived and found us a lovely spot by the racecourse. Soon after, Wilson, Adam, George, Richard, Francisco and Gareth arrived too.

There was much eating, drinking and betting by all but I think only George & Richard did any good coming out a total of £34.00 up!

After the last race at 5.15pm, we headed off into town for a drink at The Dolphin before a curry at the Ellora.

We made it just in time to catch the last train back to Reading at 22.37.



Had a very fun day!
