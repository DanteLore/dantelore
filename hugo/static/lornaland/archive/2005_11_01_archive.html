ruby tuesday...

The Good News: We've found a new quiz!
The Bad News: It's on a Tuesday!

The Westside is our new home on a Tuesday night for some quiz action. Tuesday is also "Ruby Tuesday" where you can get a curry, rice, nan and a pint for Â£4.95.

So, we arrived at the pub at 7pm, then when everyone was ordering their food, I had to leave to go to dancing arriving back just in time for the quiz to start. Not to miss out on the curry, I made sure they ordered it just before 9pm so it would be waiting for me when I got there.

The quiz was good and we didn't too badly on our first visit - 3rd place!

Better luck next week.
