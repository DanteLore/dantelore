Saturday, March 05, 2005

shopping...

With Dan off to Havant to help fix Johnson's new landrover, I decided on some retail therapy.

Armed with vouchers for Dorothy Perkins, Debenhams and House of Fraser and some cash, I hit the shops!

About 3 hours later I return with some toothpaste, dental floss and mouthwash!

Why!? Why is it when you have money you can't get anything and when you don't have money there's so many nice things you want!

Nothing seemed to fit. Everything was either too big or didn't look right. And remind me never to go shopping to Primark on a Saturday again!

Think I'll try again during the week.

Friday, March 04, 2005

aren't bosses nice...

Well, sometimes! Today my boss is very nice!

Our place is stuck in a bit of a time warp when it comes to computers. Lots of things still get done on paper and there's loads of duplication.

So, I took it upon myself to create a simple excel spreadsheet to take over from the current "paper logs". It shows all the info the old system does but has the added bonus of some nifty formula's to automatically calculate some figures.

I was quite pleased with it but my boss thinks it's the best thing since sliced bread! And today, to show how much he appreciated my hard work and what I'd done he gave me some Debenham's vouchers as a thank you. Wow!

So I am feeling very happy now :o)
