more travel adventures...

Oh dear, we've done it again! Booked holiday no.4 for this year which will actually be holiday no.3 :o)

We're off to Stockholm for the weekend at Easter, going on the 7th back on the 9th. Well, it's Dan's birthday weekend so it's his birthday present too!

We've also done some planning on holiday no.4 - our Vegas trip. We've finally decided on the route for our road trip and made some reservations for motels.

The plan...
Day 1 - Drive from Vegas, NV to Williams, AZ along Route 66.
Day 2 - Day trip to the Grand Canyon South Rim on the The Canyon Train.
 Day 3 - Drive from Williams, AZ to Monument Valley, UT. Afternoon at Monument Valley.
Day 4 - Drive from Monument Valley, UT to Page, AZ. Afternoon at Antelope Canyon.
Day 5 - Drive from Page, AZ to Springdale, UT. Afternoon at Zion National Park.
Day 6 - Drive from Springdale to Vegas, NV.

And it looks like it's gonna be pretty cheap. The accommodation for the 5 nights is about the same cost as 1 night at the Bellagio in Vegas!

grand canyon skywalk

The new Skywalk opened at the Grand Canyon today, although the public won't be allowed on it until 28th March.

The Skywalk is a horse-shoe shaped glass bridge which allows you to walk out over the GC and view the bottom, a mere 4000ft down! It's on the West Rim of the Canyon, home of the Hualapei Tribe and where we visited during our American Adventure in 2005.

We are making a trip to the Canyon during our road trip in July this year but unfortunately, it's the South Rim we're visiting this time so won't be able to go on it. Not that I'd have been able to get Dan on it! ;o)
